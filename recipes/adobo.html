Adobo

Description

Adobo originates from the Philippines and has many variations. The main trait of adobo is that it's a pork stew cooked in vinegar and soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pork (Belly recommended)
  • Potatoes
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Sear pork in pot until brown, adding salt and pepper
  2. When brown, add water just enough to almost submerge pork
  3. When water boils, add soy sauce
  4. Add vinegar 10 minutes with equal ratio to soy sauce
  5. Continue boiling on low heat until pork is fork tender around 30 mins, add laurel leaves and pork cubes 10 minutes before
  6. Add potatoes and boil until fork tender
  7. Serve with rice
